Who We Are

Atlas is a Kenyan property technology company leveraging on blockchain technology to digitize and safeguard land records through secure, tamper-proof digital archives

Atlas has a tripple mission: to secure property rights of bina fide land owners by preserving the integrity of critical land documents, to promote a seamless access to land records, and to deepen due diligence rigour in Kenya

By proactively using technology, Atlas aims to restore trust in Kenya's real estate market - a sector long plaqued by opacity and fraud

Atlas platform leverages the decentralization and immutabillity (tamper-proof nature) of blockchain to offer a more secure and accessible future for land ownership verification in Kenya and beyond.

Create a secure digital archive for verified land records on the blockchain to ensure records integrity

Provide an alternative point of reference for land due diligence

Employing technology to improve general due diligence experience & rigour

Break the stranglehold by GoK on land registries which gives rise to contagion risk

Complement various government sponsored (GoK) land registries, but also keep them accountable

Provide digital redundancy (backup) to GoK registries

Sensitize the public on how to comply with precedent setting rulings by the Supreme Court of Kenya on land DD
