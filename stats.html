Atlas Land Registry FAQ

Secure, Transparent, Blockchain-based Land Titles

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Atlas Blockchain Solutions? ▶

Atlas is Kenya’s first blockchain-based digital land registry, providing immutable and verifiable land records.

How does Atlas ensure the security of land titles? ▶

Through decentralized blockchain technology that makes land records tamper-proof, timestamped, and verifiable.

What are the benefits over traditional registries? ▶

Fraud prevention, transparency, faster due diligence, audit trails, and better protection of property rights.

How can I verify my title on Atlas? ▶

Use the ‘Verify’ service and input your title deed details to get secure blockchain-backed verification.

Does Atlas help resolve disputes? ▶

Yes. You can raise disputes directly on the platform for review by government registrars and legal authorities.

How do I register or enlist my property? ▶

Use the 'Enlist' service to upload property information and anchor your title securely on the blockchain.

Is Atlas a replacement for the government registry? ▶

No, it complements the official registry by providing enhanced verification, audit logs, and transparency.

Can Atlas facilitate property transfers? ▶

Yes, property ownership can be securely transferred using the 'Transfer' feature built into the platform.

How do I get started with Atlas? ▶

Create a verified account (with KYC), purchase credit, and use the available services based on your needs.
