Who We Are

Atlas is a Kenyan property technology company leveraging on blockchain technology to secure land records.

Atlas seeks to secure property rights of bona fide landowners, promote access to land records, and deepen due diligence rigour in Kenya.

We are proactively using technology to improve the convenience and rigour of land due diligence, to restore trust in Kenya’s real estate market which suffers from an endemic trust deficit.

Atlas is banking on the decentralization (wide accessibility) and immutability (tamper-proof nature) of blockchain technology to secure land records in Kenya and beyond.

As a Proptech, Atlas is effectively an electronic land registry which is anchored in law under Land Registration Act & Land Act 2012.

Create a secure digital archive for verified land records on the blockchain to ensure records integrity

Provide an alternative point of reference for land due diligence

Employing technology to improve general due diligence experience & rigour

Break the stranglehold by GoK on land registries which gives rise to contagion risk

Complement various government sponsored (GoK) land registries, but also keep them accountable

Provide digital redundancy (backup) to GoK registries

Sensitize the public on how to comply with precedent setting rulings by the Supreme Court of Kenya on land DD
