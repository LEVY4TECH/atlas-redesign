Bank on Blockchain,
Secure your Land & Legacy

Atlas is Kenya's first blockchain-powered platform that allows landowners to create a digital archive of their land records backed on the blockchain.

Landowners are able to digitize, verify, and secure their land records with unmatched transparency and security while the public is empowered to conduct due diligence in the most convenient & rigorous way.

Weak property rights

Atlas is here to secure property rights of bona fide landowners by
preserving the integrity of critical land documents through digital
archives backed on the blockchain. Manual registries have rendered
land records vulnerable to manipulation to the detriment of genuine owners.

Cumbersome access to land records

Atlas is keen to promote access to land records in a secure,
transparent and convenient manner. This convenience is geared at
improving transparency around land ownership in the country.

Superficial land due diligence

Atlas is here to deepen due diligence rigour in Kenya by providing the requisite information supporting land ownership on demand basis and in a convenient manner.
